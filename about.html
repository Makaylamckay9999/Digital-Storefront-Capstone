Our story

Boba Time was established in 2025 on the Mississippi Gulf Coast in Gulfport. Inspired by local cafes and a focus on bubble milk teas and smoothies. Our brand first came into place around 2019, from one of our founders inspired from the communal presence of cafes and the variety we commited our dreams to developing Boba-Time. Our Bubble teas and smoothies are made fresh daily with high quality indegrients , innovation, quick serivce, and products made with care. Whether you're on the go, looking for a hang out spot for friends, or curiosty Boba-Time is here for you!
